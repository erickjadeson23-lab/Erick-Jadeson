Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before we work together

Why should I hire you instead of doing marketing myself?

Book marketing is a full-time job that requires expertise, time, and constant adaptation to changing algorithms and platforms. While you focus on writing your next book, I handle the complex work of reaching readers, optimizing your presence, and driving sales. My data-driven strategies and proven track record mean you get results faster than trial-and-error approaches.

Why is everything done through email?

Email provides complete transparency and accountability. Every decision, strategy, and result is documented in writing. This creates a clear trail of our work together, protects both of us, and ensures nothing gets lost in conversation. You can reference any discussion anytime, and it respects your schedule—you respond when convenient.

What genres do you work with?

I work with authors across all genres—fiction and non-fiction. From romance to thriller, fantasy to business books, each genre requires different marketing approaches. I customize strategies based on your specific genre's reader behavior, platform preferences, and market dynamics.

How quickly will I see results?

Results vary based on your starting point and goals. Book launches show immediate results during launch week. Email list building typically shows meaningful growth in 30-60 days. Amazon optimization can improve rankings in 2-4 weeks. Long-term platform building takes 90+ days but creates sustainable growth. I set clear expectations in our initial strategy discussion.

Do you work with debut authors?

Absolutely! I love working with debut authors who are serious about building their career from the start. Starting with professional marketing from day one sets you up for long-term success rather than trying to fix problems later. Many of my most successful clients started as debut authors.

What is your response time?

I guarantee response within 24 hours on business days (Monday-Friday). In practice, my average response time is 8 hours. For urgent matters during active campaigns, I typically respond within 2-4 hours. You'll never be left wondering about the status of your project.
